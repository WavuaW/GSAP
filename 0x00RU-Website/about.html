About Me

I am a historian of race and medicine, a digital content creator, and a champion for Black women's health, education, and empowerment. At the core of my varied passions is creating a world where Black women around the world can confidently and joyfully pursue their wildest dreams.

My research focuses on the historical intersections of race, gender, and medicine, with a particular emphasis on the unique health challenges faced by Black women. I'm dedicated to amplifying the voices and stories of Black women, and to ensuring that they have access to the resources and support they need to thrive.

As a digital content creator, I use my platform to educate, inspire, and empower Black women. Through my blog, social media channels, and other online initiatives, I share my insights, experiences, and resources designed to help Black women navigate the complexities of life, career, and wellness.

At the heart of my work is a deep commitment to creating a more just, equitable, and compassionate world for Black women. I believe in the power of community, collaboration, and collective action to drive meaningful change.
